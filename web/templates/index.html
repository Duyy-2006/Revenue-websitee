Stats API

YummyTrackStat per-account stats via Bearer token at yummytrackstat\token.txt. Auto-checked every 20 min — if it shows expired, re-copy the token (no restart needed). Endpoints: /api/trackstat/<game> · /api/trackstat/all (all your games, stable) · /api/trackstat/active (only games running now).

ZP ZeroSolver

ZeroSolver checking…

Every 20 min, accounts with a CAPTCHA error (assigned to a device, live cookie) are submitted to ZeroSolver's in-game solver. Accounts already in the queue are skipped; once their job finishes, still-captcha accounts are re-sent next cycle. Only successful solves are billed (~$0.0025 each). Key at Zp auto faceunlock\api_solver.txt.
